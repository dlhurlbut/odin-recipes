Denice's Sore Throat Magic Potion

Description

Listen, sometimes we all wake up with that bit of sore throat that isn't dire enough to miss work or go to the doctor over, but still really sucks. After years of people recommending many different rememdies to me, I discovered that the most surefire bet was to use them all at once. It's not gentle, by any means, but if you're willing to push through the process of drinking it, it always does the trick.

Ingredients

  • about 1c boiling water in a mug
  • 1tbsp honey
  • 2 splashes of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 splash of lemon juice
  • 1 splash of lime juice
  • a hearty sprinkle of cinnamon
  • a slightly less hearty sprinkle of cayenne
  • a couple drops of DoTerra brand On Guard oil blend
  • fortitude

Steps

  1. Mix everytning into the mug of hot water.
  2. Sip carefully.
  3. Decide whether or not having or sore throat or drinking this infernal concoction would be worse.
  4. Live with your choice.
